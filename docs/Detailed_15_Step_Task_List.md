# Policy Placement Workflow: Detailed 15-Step Task List

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive breakdown of all 15 steps required to execute the Advantage annuity structure with SMA deployment for the Client's domestic LLC. Each step includes owners, timing, prerequisites, and current status where applicable.

---

## Phase 0: Coordination & Preparation (Steps 1-4)

### Step 1: Confirm the Client and advisers want to move forward
**Status**: ✅ Done  
**Owners**: TPBC, Stephen  
**Activities**:
- Illustration accepted
- Client confirmation received
- Advisers aligned on moving forward

**Outcome**: Authorization to proceed with application process

---

### Step 2: Gather all data and documents for the Client and domestic LLC
**Status**: 🔄 In Process  
**Owners**: TPBC, Stephen  
**Activities**:
- TPBC compiles and organizes all incoming information
- Client identification and address verification
- Trust documents (if applicable)
- LLC membership agreement
- Source of funds statements
- All required KYC/AML documentation

**Outcome**: Complete documentation package ready for application

---

### Step 3: Ensure Stephen has approved discretionary account agreement for Advantage
**Status**: ⚡ Immediate Priority  
**Owners**: TPBC, Stephen, Advantage (DeAnn Diaz)  
**Activities**:
- Obtain approved discretionary account agreement for Advantage
- Ensure Stephen receives approval for "no client control" document
- Review and confirm all agreement terms
- Prepare for execution

**Outcome**: Discretionary agreement approved, no-client-control document approved

---

### Step 4: Build the account opening plan
**Status**: ⚡ Immediate Priority  
**Owners**: TPBC, Sam (3cStructures)  
**Key Participants**: Stephen, Dave Wheeler (Axonic)  
**Activities**:
- Coordinate between Stephen, Dave Wheeler (Axonic), and BNY Mellon
- Define account structure requirements
- Confirm custodian capabilities (BNY Mellon)
- Plan BNYM feed integration for Axonic investments
- Establish coordination timeline

**Outcome**: Comprehensive account opening plan finalized and communicated to all parties

## Phase 1: Application & Compliance (Steps 5-8)

### Step 5: Establish secure data link
**Status**: ⚡ Immediate Priority  
**Target**: Monday or Tuesday  
**Owners**: Sam (3cStructures), Advantage (DeAnn Diaz)  
**Activities**:
- Sam coordinates with Advantage to create secure pipeline
- Establish encrypted data transfer between TPBC and Advantage
- Upload all due diligence materials to secure portal
- Confirm Advantage receipt of materials

**Outcome**: Secure data pipeline operational, all materials uploaded and confirmed

---

### Step 6: Prepare DocuSign package
**Status**: Ready to Start Monday  
**Target Delivery**: Wednesday  
**Owners**: Heath Miller (3cStructures), TPBC, Sam (3cStructures)  
**Needs**: Employment details and final data elements  
**Activities**:
- Heath Miller prepares DocuSign package
- Include annuity application
- Include all required supporting documents
- Route DocuSign so TPBC and DeAnn Diaz are final stops
- Obtain employment details for completion
- Gather final data elements

**Timeline**:
- Start: Monday
- Target Completion: Wednesday

**Outcome**: DocuSign package complete and ready for client execution

---

### Step 7: the Client signs DocuSign
**Target**: Thursday or Friday  
**Primary Owner**: Heath Miller (3cStructures)  
**Support**: Sam (3cStructures)  
**Prerequisites**: Step 6 completed  
**Activities**:
- Heath Miller oversees the Client DocuSign execution
- Client signs annuity application
- Client signs all supporting documents
- Heath routes signed documents to PR counsel
- PR counsel reviews and approves (3cStructures oversight)
- Heath forwards approved documents to Advantage

**Workflow**:
1. the Client executes DocuSign
2. Documents → PR counsel
3. PR counsel review (3cStructures oversight)
4. Documents → Advantage (DeAnn Diaz)

**Outcome**: Signed application and documents delivered to Advantage

---

### Step 8: Advantage compliance approval
**Target**: Friday or Monday (following Step 7 and Step 5)  
**Owner**: DeAnn Diaz (Advantage - Client Services Manager)  
**Prerequisites**:
- Step 7 completed (signed application received)
- Step 5 completed (due diligence materials uploaded)

**Activities**:
- DeAnn Diaz reviews complete submission
- Advantage compliance team conducts full compliance review
- Verify all documentation complete and accurate
- Clear submission for processing
- Issue approval to proceed

**Outcome**: Compliance approval granted, case cleared for policy placement

## Transition: Account Opening & Funding (Steps 9-12)

### Step 9: Advantage opens policy account at BNY Mellon
**Target**: Friday  
**Primary Owner**: DeAnn Diaz (Advantage)  
**Coordinators**: Dave Wheeler (Axonic), TPBC, Stephen  
**Support**: Sam (3cStructures) - technical coordination  
**Prerequisites**:
- Step 7 completed (signed documents)
- Step 5 completed (secure pipeline and materials)

**Activities**:
- DeAnn oversees execution of account-opening documents
- Dave Wheeler (Axonic) coordinates BNY Mellon setup
- Open policy account at BNY Mellon
- Establish BNYM feeds for Axonic investments
- Configure account access and permissions
- Sam provides technical coordination support

**Outcome**: BNY Mellon policy account opened and operational

---

### Step 10: Execution of discretionary management agreement and "no client control"
**Owner**: DeAnn Diaz (Advantage), Stephen  
**Prerequisite**: Step 9 completed (account opened)  
**Activities**:
- DeAnn Diaz signs Stephen's discretionary management agreement
- Stephen signs "no client control" affirmation
- Execute all required agreements
- File executed documents

**Outcome**: Discretionary management agreement in force, no-client-control affirmed

---

### Step 11: Greenlight to place wire
**Owners**: DeAnn Diaz (Advantage), Stephen  
**Prerequisites**:
- Step 10 completed (agreements executed)
- Step 9 completed (account opened)
- Step 8 completed (compliance approved)

**Activities**:
- DeAnn and Stephen issue approval to fund the account
- DeAnn sends wiring instructions to Stephen and TPBC
- Stephen coordinates with the Client for wire transfer
- Client wires funds per instructions

**Outcome**: Greenlight issued, wiring instructions distributed, wire in process

---

### Step 12: Funds received
**Owner**: Advantage (DeAnn Diaz)  
**Prerequisite**: Step 11 completed (wire sent)  
**Activities**:
- Funds arrive at AIBC (Advantage's bank)
- Advantage retains administrative charges
- Advantage wires remaining balance to BNY Mellon account
- Confirm funds received at BNY Mellon

**Workflow**:
1. Client → AIBC (Advantage's bank)
2. Advantage retains charges
3. Advantage → BNY Mellon account

**Outcome**: Funds fully transferred and available for investment
